                              Paper Cups By Arihant


                              We manufacture a wide range of paper cups in various shapes and sizes from 3 Oz to 20 Oz, Ripple paper cups /Double wall paper cups/ with plastic lids. We provide paper cups in the following three categories :


                              • Single wall paper cups
                              • Double wall paper cups
                              • Triple wall paper cups


                              Single Wall Paper Cups


                              Available in a range of sizes from 4oz through to 16oz. Our premium grade single wall plain white and printed Enjoy cups are suited to the needs of many food service operators. In addition to our generic stock lines we also offer bespoke printing.

                              • Custom print up to six colours
                              • Madefrom high quality food grade paper board.
                              • Each cup is printed with specific batch coding so we can easily trace the cup back through the manufacturing process.




                              Double Wall Paper Cups


                              Available in a range of sizes from 8oz to 16oz. Our Double Wall cups have a smooth matt finish offering better heat insulation than our premium single-wall cup.

                              • Custom print up to six colours
                              • Madefrom high quality food grade paper board.
                              • Each cup is printed with specific batch coding so we can easily trace the cup back through the manufacturing process.




                              Triple Wall Paper Cups


                              Available in a range of sizes from 4oz to 20oz. This cup is available in four different designs, from the natural un-bleached Kraft through to the more contemporary black, white or classic ‘enjoy’ design. The triple-wall ripple cup provides the best heat insulation as the unique design includes a corrugated paper wrap which offers increased hand-comfort, heat insulation and premium brand presentation.

                              • Custom print up to six colours
                              • Madefrom high quality food grade paper board.
                              • Each cup is printed with specific batch coding so we can easily trace the cup back through the manufacturing process.
